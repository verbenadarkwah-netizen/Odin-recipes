Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made of wide pasta sheets layered with rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy cheeses, and aromatic herbs. Baked until golden and bubbling, it delivers a comforting blend of savory flavors and satisfying textures.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

STEPS TO FOLLOW;

  • Cook beef and onions in a large skillet until browned.
  • Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar, basil, salt, and pepper.
  • Simmer for 15 minutes.
  • Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with eggs and salt.
  • Layer noodles, meat sauce, and ricotta mixture in a baking dish.
  • Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
  • Bake for 25 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
